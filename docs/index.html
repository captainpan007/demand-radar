How It Works

From Noise to Opportunity in 3 Steps

📡

Scan

Scrapes top developer communities daily — Hacker News, Product Hunt, IndieHackers — collecting hundreds of posts and discussions.

🧠

Analyze

AI scores each signal across 4 dimensions: pain level, payment signal, executability, and reach. Low-quality items are filtered out.

📊

Report

Generates a clean, interactive HTML report with demand summaries, build estimates, and tool plans — ready to act on.
